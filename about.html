About Jammr

Jammr is a web-based music player inspired by Spotify. It brings you a handpicked playlist of royalty-free NCS (No Copyright Sounds) tracks, allowing seamless playback, progress tracking, and song navigation. Whether you're studying, working, or chilling, Jammr keeps the vibe alive with smooth controls and a minimal interface.

Developed as a fun frontend project, Jammr showcases basic features like:

  • Responsive UI with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Play/Pause, Next/Previous song controls
  • Dynamic song list and progress bar
  • Visually engaging GIF-based animation

More features coming soon, including playlists, search, and custom themes!
